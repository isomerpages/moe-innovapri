<h2><strong>Safety and Well‑Being at Innova Primary School</strong></h2>
<h4><strong>Safety Vision</strong></h4>
<p>A safe and secure school where staff and students champion and take responsibility
for their physical and psychological safety and well‑being, as well as
that of the community.</p>
<h4><strong>Safety Mission</strong></h4>
<p>To provide a safe, respectful and inviting environment for students to
learn and staff to work.</p>
<hr>
<h4><strong>Our Commitment to Safety</strong></h4>
<p>At Innova Primary School, the safety and well‑being of our students and
staff is of paramount importance. We are committed to fostering a <strong>safe, caring and supportive school environment</strong> where
everyone feels protected, respected and valued.</p>
<p>We adopt a <strong>whole‑school approach to safety</strong>, which includes
physical safety, cyber safety, and psychological well‑being. Through proactive
measures, clear procedures and shared responsibility, we strive to ensure
that safety is an integral part of everyday school life.</p>
<hr>
<h4><strong>Key Safety Focus Areas</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Physical Safety</strong>
<br>Ensuring a safe school environment through regular safety checks, safe
practices during lessons and school activities, and preparedness for emergencies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Psychological Safety and Well‑Being</strong>
<br>Promoting a caring and inclusive culture where students feel emotionally
safe, supported and respected, and where positive relationships are nurtured.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Cyber Safety</strong>
<br>Guiding students to use digital technologies responsibly and safely, and
to develop good online habits.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Student and Staff Responsibility</strong>
<br>Encouraging students and staff to take ownership of their own safety and
to look out for the well‑being of others.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h4><strong>Working Together for a Safe School</strong></h4>
<p>Safety is a shared responsibility. We work closely with <strong>parents and community partners</strong> to
reinforce safety awareness and to support our students in developing safe
habits both in and out of school.</p>
<p>Through education, vigilance and care, Innova Primary School remains committed
to providing a safe and conducive environment for learning and growth.</p>
