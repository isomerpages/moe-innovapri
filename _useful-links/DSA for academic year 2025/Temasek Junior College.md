<p>Temasek Junior College is a 6-year institution offering IP1 (Sec 1) to
JC2.&nbsp; We are happy to invite your Primary 6 students and their parents
(<em>P5 also welcome!</em>) to our upcoming <strong>TJC</strong>  <strong>Integrated Programme Open House:</strong>
</p>
<p>Date:&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sat 27 April (end of Week 6)</p>
<p>Time: &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 9am – 1pm</p>
<p>Venue: &nbsp;2 Tampines Ave 9, S529564 (Temporary site till Jan 2028,
when we return to the brand-new Rebuilt Bedok Campus at 2 Bedok South Road)
(if no delays!).</p>
<p>At the Open House, your students can find out more about our unique programme
offerings, through which we nurture outstanding students in diverse areas,
many of whom have gone on to achieve excellent outcomes in and beyond our
College. &nbsp;</p>
<p>We append our Open House e-flier for your students’ easy reference, and
also attach it for your ease of forwarding.&nbsp; We would be grateful
if you could share it with colleagues / students / parents as you see fit.</p>
