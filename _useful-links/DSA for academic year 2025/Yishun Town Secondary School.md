<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Yishun Town Secondary School (YTSS) will be
holding our E-Open Day 2024 to share with prospective students, parents
and teachers more about our school, our interesting programmes and our
DSA opportunities at YTSS. We will be hosting this session virtually via
a Zoom Webinar.</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; We would like to warmly invite your students
and their parents to connect with us and find out more about our school,
our people and our programmes. Join us to learn more about what makes life
at YTSS so special!</p>
